What is a ShareCard

Information,
tied to a place.

A ShareCard is a note, tip, update, or insight — attached to a specific location. It's not a review. It's a message for the right person, at the right place, at the right time.

📍 Attached to a place
✍️ Written by someone you know
🌍 Shareable when you choose

How it works

Three steps.
That's it.

No forms. No star ratings. No lengthy reviews.
